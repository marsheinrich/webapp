Intuit QuickBooks Online Integration
The first step is to authorize ezClocker to access your QuickBooks Online company data.
ezClocker will send you to the QuickBooks Online authorization page at Intuit where you will perform the following tasks:
  1. Sign in to your Intuit QuickBooks Online account (if you are not already signed in).
  2. Select the QuickBooks Online company you wish to connect to ezClocker (if you have more than one)
  3. Verify that it is ezClocker asking permission to acess your QuickBooks company file.
  4. Click the Authorize button to complete the process.
After authorization, Intuit will then send you back to ezClocker automatically.
Click Authorize to visit Intuit QuickBooks Online to authenticate and authorize ezClocker access.
