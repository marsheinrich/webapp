Intuit QuickBooks Online Integration
Connecting ezClocker to Intuit's QuickBooks Online product will allow you to push your employee's total time for a selected date range (or pay period) to your Intuit QuickBooks Online account.
Pushing a pay period's total time to QuickBooks avoids the need to manually enter total time data for each employee when you or your accountant is processing your payroll.
