QuickBooks Integration

ezClocker will now step you through mapping employees in QuickBooks to the employees in ezClocker. The steps to complete:

  1. Log into QuickBooks and give permission for ezClocker to exchange data.
  2. Match QuickBooks employees to your ezClocker employees.
Click next to log into QuickBooks and provide permission for ezClocker to exchange data.
