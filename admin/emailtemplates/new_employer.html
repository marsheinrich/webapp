Welcome to ezClocker!

As an employer, you can view, clock in/out employees, add/delete missing time entries, and you can download the time sheets for a pay period as a .csv file so you can easily send it to your payroll manager. If you want a quick overview of how ezClocker works please watch our demo video here

A couple of things to keep in mind as you get started:
  • Your employer account is different than an employee account. If you want to create an employee account for yourself you currently need to use a different email address than the one used for your employer account.
  • Invite your employees to use ezClocker from the Employer Dashboard on the website or the mobile phone app.
  • Your ezClocker employer account allows you to access your data from your mobile devices or computer.

Employee Invite Process

Inviting employees to ezClocker allows them to use the website or our native iOS or Andriod apps to clock in or out of their shifts. The following steps occur when you add/invite an employee:

  • The employee clicks the email link taking them to the employee account creation page.
  • On the account creation page, your employee enters their password and clicks the Sign Up button.
Please instruct your employees to not download and register within the ezclocker mobile app until they have received the invite. Doing so might result in them creating an employer account and not an employee account.
